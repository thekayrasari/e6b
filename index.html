VECTOR DIAGRAM GUIDE

The physical canvas models the real-world aerodynamics of flight:
True Heading ($TH$): The direction the aircraft points (thrust force vector).
Wind Vector: Environmental force blowing the plane off-course (orange flowlines).
Ground Track (Course): The actual path traveled over the ground ($GS$ vector).
